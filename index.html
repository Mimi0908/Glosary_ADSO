Chutes and Ladders

Here you can practice and remember the words from the previous glossary in a board game.

Rules:

They are the same rules as the classic ladders and snakes game, you roll a die and advance; only that when you land on a snake square, you have the opportunity to answer a question correctly (this will be a question of comparison or choosing the correct word) if you answer correctly, the token will not fall and it will advance 1 position, if you answer wrong it falls for the snake.
By landing on the boxes with circles, which by answering a question correctly will allow you to get ahead.
And the boxes with squares that when you answer wrong, you will go back 1 box.
The winner is the one who reaches the star first.
